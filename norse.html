Rune Decipher

Runes, ancient symbols of mystique and magic, have woven a captivating tale throughout history. These enigmatic characters, forming runic alphabets known as "futharks," held unique powers of communication and divination for the Norse and Germanic cultures. What adds to their intrigue is the revelation that not all modern letters existed in these ancient alphabets.
